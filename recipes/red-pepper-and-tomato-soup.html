Red Pepper and Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 6 red peppers, halved and deseeded
  • 1 kg of ripe vine tomatoes, halved and cores removed
  • 3 tbsp virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp of dried oregano
  • 800ml vegetable stock
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp tomto puree
  • Fresh basil - optional

Method

  1. Place prepared peppers and tomatoes in an air fryer and drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt, pepper and oregano, heat until roasted.
  2. Place 800ml of water into a large saucepan and add a vegetable stock cube.
  3. Place roasted vegetables, tomato puree, paprika and reamining oil into the stock, bring to the boil and gently simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Ladle mixture into a blender and blend until smooth, serve with bread and optional fresh basil seasoning.
