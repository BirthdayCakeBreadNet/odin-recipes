Banana and Peanut Butter Smmothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 400ml almond milk
  • 1 tbsp runny peanut butter
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Cinnamon - optional

Steps

  1. Place ingredients into a blender or food processor.
  2. Blend until smooth and serve in a tall glass.
  3. Optionally, sprinkle with cinnamon before enjoying.
