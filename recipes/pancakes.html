Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300ml almond milk
  • 1 tbsp of dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp vegetable or sunflower oil - extra for frying
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • Caster sugar for serving
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Steps

  1. Place plain flour, eggs, milk and oil into a bowl and whisk into a smooth batter.
  2. Set aside mixture for 30 minutes to rest.
  3. Preheat a medium sized crepe pan, once hot, pour in enough mixture to cover pan and cook on both sides until golden.
  4. Serve with caster sugar and lemon, or use your own filling if you prefer.
